> **Method & honesty notes.** All numbers are computed directly from the export by script (`deep_analyze.py`, `fix_counts.py`), not estimated. The chat is **Manglish (Malayalam in Latin script) + Hindi + English**, so emotion/phrase detection uses multilingual keyword lexicons — directional, not perfect. Two parsing traps were found and corrected:
> 1. **"ilu"** in Malayalam is the locative suffix *"-il / in"* ("office ilu" = *in office*), **not** "I love you" — removed from love counts.
> 2. **"motta"** is Malayalam for **egg** ("motta curry" = egg curry) and **"motta kunju"** is the couple's pet name. A single playful spam-flood on **28 May 2026** ("MOTTA KUNJU SE MERA PYAR HOGAYA" repeated 300+×, plus "para"/"hycross" filler) inflates raw token counts — reported using **distinct-message** counts where it matters.
